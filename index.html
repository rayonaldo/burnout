It is estimated that approximately 25 percent of fresh fruit and vegetable produce is thrown away each year in Australia due to visual imperfection, contributing to global food waste. To help combat this food supply issue, grocers and supermarkets have developed new marketing campaigns. For example Sydney’s Harris Farm have developed a campaign called ‘Imperfect Picks’ and Woolworths have introduced ‘The Odd Bunch’, which provide consumers with fresh and healthy fruits and vegetables that might not look perfect, at a cheaper price.
